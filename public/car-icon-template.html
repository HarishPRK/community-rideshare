Instructions to use this car icon:

  1. Take screenshots of the icon versions above:
    • Take a screenshot of the circular icon (recommended for the favicon)
    • Make sure to capture just the icon without any surrounding white space
  2. Convert the screenshots to favicon and app icons:
    • Use an online converter like favicon.io to convert your screenshots
    • Upload your screenshot to generate favicon.ico, logo192.png, and logo512.png
    • Download the generated icons package
  3. Replace the existing files in your project:
    • Replace public/favicon.ico with the new favicon.ico
    • Replace public/logo192.png with the new logo192.png
    • Replace public/logo512.png with the new logo512.png
  4. Update the browser:
    • Clear your browser cache (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R)
    • Restart your React development server (if running)
    • Reload the application to see the new car icon in the browser tab

Note: You've already updated the manifest.json file to match the Community Rideshare app name and theme color, which was an essential step.

Once you've completed these steps, the React logo in the browser tab will be replaced with your new car logo, giving your Community Rideshare application a more appropriate and branded appearance!
